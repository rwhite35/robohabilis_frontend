Seeding Business Success With The Right Solutions.

Let's face it, your IT staff is stretched thin and “the business” has no shortage of new feature requests. Delivering software solutions on-time/on budget is made harder by the ever-necessary “legacy maintenance”. IT managers face a hard reality - demand always out weighs capacity( time and resources ).

Robohabilis delivers end-to-end software projects and services. Typical projects include “make-to-order” custom business tools for enterprise to middleware solutions for systems interoperability. Handier still, Robohabilis levers cloud solutions for realtime project management and virtual team collaboration.

Seeing The Forest For The Trees

Business runs a dizzying array of technologies that communicate internally and externally with other systems. Today’s business technology is interconnected and required to seamlessly integrate with different protocols and platforms.

Robohabilis is technology/platform neutral. While open source is our bailiwick of choice, that does not mean it’s the de-facto recommendation for your project. Our toolbox is diverse and without bias for any technology or platform. And frankly in todays cloud based world, the how is less important than how well it performs. And that's what we care about most, high performance solutions that are lightning fast and bullet proof.

Nice To Haves Are Our Must Haves

How many times has a major/minor update broken functionality? Worse yet, the severity level required a roll back to the last ‘stable version’. Besides the obvious pain, management's confidence and team morale drags. And usually the root cause is not enough time and resources to complete a project without skipping steps.

Robohabilis avoids that unpleasantness through Test Driven Development (TDD) and automated performance testing. Quality Assurance is engaged right from the get go. And on release night we’ll be in the scrum testing out each use case and checking off the project's criteria for acceptance.
