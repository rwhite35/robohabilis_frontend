Seeding Business Success With The Right Digital Tools.

Lets face it, your IT staff is stretched thin and “the business” has no shortage of new feature requests. Delivering software solutions on-time/on budget is made harder by the ever-necessary “legacy maintenance”. Todays IT department faces a hard reality - demand out weighs capacity( time and resources ).

Robohabilis’ provides “make-to-order” custom business tools as your end-to-end engineering and development software service provider. Services include meeting with project stakeholders, requirements gathering and Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) management.

The Right Technology
For The Job

Business leverages a dizzying array of technologies that run on every platform and required to communicate internally and externally with other systems. Today’s technology is scalable, seamlessly connected and available 24/7 365.

Robohabilis is technology neutral. While open source is preferred, that does not mean it’s the defacto recommendation for your project. Our toolbox is diverse and without bias for any technology or platform. And frankly in todays cloud based business world, the how is less important than how well it performs. Performance is our priority and how we measure success.

Nice To Haves Are
Our Must Haves

How many times has an major/minor update or patch broken functionality? Then finding and hot fixing the errant code is a resource sucking time sink. Worse yet, the severity level required a roll back to the last ‘stable version’. Besides the obvious pain of all that, confidence in the department is eroded and team morale drags.

Robohabilis avoids that noise through Test Driven Development (TDD) and automated testing solutions. In many ways we are compelled to deliver an “all green” solution because our next project is on the line. And on release night we’ll be there, in the scrum, testing out use cases and checking off each projects acceptance criteria.
